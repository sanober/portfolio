About

Hi, I'm Sanober Meher, and I graduated from the University of Urdu (FUUAST) in 2013 with a degree in Computer Science (BSCS).

I previously worked at Meezan Bank Limited as Software Engineer, I worked on a lot of projects over there including Bike Ijara.

I built the project with one other engineers over the period of three months on a .Net framework. I left my company in 2018 and became a housewife as I wanted to takecare of my family and work as a freelancer, but I found really hard to get freelance stuff on .Net.

Now over all these years I have grown my interests in Front End Engineering, and I love to create beautiful and performant products with delightful user experiences.

I'm interested in the Front End Engineering, because I like working in this ridesharing space and creating products to improve the lives of users.

If you have any queries or want to hire me, feel free to drop a message at sanober.meher@gmail.com
